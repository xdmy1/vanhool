Our Journey

Our Story

Inter Bus was founded with a simple mission: to provide Inter Bus bus operators and maintenance professionals with reliable access to genuine parts and components.

Understanding the challenges of maintaining older Inter Bus vehicles, we've built an extensive network of suppliers and maintain a comprehensive inventory of both current and discontinued parts.

From brake systems and air pressure components to electrical parts and body panels, we ensure your Inter Bus buses stay on the road safely and efficiently.
